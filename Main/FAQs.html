FAQ's
How do I Track My Order ?
You'll be provided with a link to a tracking page to your email, within 2 hours of the order being dispatched. At the time of delivery, you shall be contacted by the courier agent. In case you lost the link or don't have access to the mail, feel free to contact our customer care for assistance.
How Do I Place an Order ?
You can add items through your cart, either from the product page, or the home and collection pages. Quantities can be adjusted while viewing your cart, or directly from the product page. You can then proceed to the checkout segment, and add relevant shipping and billing details. You can pay via multiple payment options, like net banking, UPI, credit or debit card etc. on our secure payment gateways.
Do I Need To Create Account To Place Order ?
No, Accounts are Not Necessary to Place an Order. However, in order to efficiently utilize our platform, and give us a chance to serve you better, we strongly recommend creating an account while making a purchase.
How Long Will it Take To Get My Package?
Delivery times depend upon a variety of factors. The average courier time for a package to get delivered from the order date is 4 days. In cases in which the shipping address is Tamilnadu, you can expect delivery within the 4-5 day. For pin-codes, that are either remote or rural, you can expect delivery in 5-6 working days.
